Introducing Speakers



Tammy Tibbets

President and Founder of She's The First

Tammy is the first in her family to graduate from college–she studied journalism at The College of New Jersey, where her final assignment unexpectedly put her on the path to social entrepreneurship. After reporting on a foundation that sponsored children’s education in war-torn Liberia, she felt the urgent need to give girls worldwide access to an education. This was a story she could not walk away from as an unbiased reporter, so she traveled to Liberia and began to volunteer for the cause after graduation.

Jancy

2013 Scholar from India

Meet Jancy, an 18-year-old college sophomore in India! After graduating from Shanti Bhavan, Jancy is attending Jyoti Nivas College where she is studying Econ-Tourism and singing in the choir! Jancy is also a fantastic artist and dreams of being a fashion designer – she was the judge of last year’s Tie-dye Cupcake Bake-off T-shirt design contest! She also is a master at henna. Jancy is the first girl in her family to have the opportunity to go to school and to learn to read and write.
